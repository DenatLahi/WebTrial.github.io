About

Mateusz Piesiak

Mateusz Piesiak is a nature photographer from Wroclaw, Poland. Throught the the year he documents hidden life of animals and shows the beauty of nature to others.

He's photographs has been awarded in international competitions including Wildlife Photographer of the Year and published in various magazines.
